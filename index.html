Open Charlotte Brigade

Open Charlotte Brigade, our volunteer army which began in 2014 as Code for Charlotte, uses its time and talent to make Charlotte a more open and accessible community.

About the Brigade

Advocacy

Open Charlotte advocates for Open Data, Open Government and Open Access. Our programs such as "Data and Donuts" and "Open Data Day" introduce people to Open Data concepts.

Education

Civic Engagement begins with education. Through workshops and speaker series, Open Charlotte seeks to educate on how to become engaged in our local community.
