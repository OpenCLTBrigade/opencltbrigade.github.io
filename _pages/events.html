Hack for Change

Hack for Change is a big one day civic design-a-thon Open Charlotte holds to celebrate National Day of Civic Hacking. On this day, organizations all over the nation bring together community members and civic leaders to work with our local governments and tackle some our toughest challenges. We'll have more details on our 2019 event later this year!
