Our Leadership Team

Open Charlotte Brigade is a volunteer-driven organization and we invite anyone to help us grow the civic tech movement or help steer our efforts in doing so. Our core leadership team is made up of volunteers who are the most familiar and engaged in driving individual areas of our brigade.

Executive Team

Our executive team oversees operations and the general direction of our brigade, creating programs and partnerships to help the organization achieve its vision.

Technology Leads

Our technology leads are volunteers that are dedicated to overseeing Open Charlotte's technical infrastructure and providing technical assistance to project teams. While we try to keep our technical projects language-agnostic, tech leads may suggest technologies or best practices to maximize a project's success.

Ryan Edge

Marketing Team

Our marketing team members are evangelists of the civic tech movement and use their storytelling skills to update our social media channels with civic tech updates, blog about what we're working on, and even help plan events.
